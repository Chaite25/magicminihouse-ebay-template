Dollhouse Scale. What do I have/What size should I buy?

  • Each dollhouse scale represents a proportional relationship between the miniature and its real-life counterpart.

Here are the details for the specified scales:

  • 1:6 Scale (Play scale or Fashion Doll Scale). In this scale, 1 inch corresponds to 6 inches in full size. Adult dolls are typically around 12 inches (30.5 centimeters) tall in this scale.
  • Commonly used for fashion dolls like Blythe, and collectible action figures.
  • 1:12 Scale (One Inch to One Foot). Widely accepted among collectors of artisan miniatures.
  • Most adult dolls are depicted between 5 inches (12.7 centimeters) and 6 inches (15.2 centimeters) in height. Commonly used for OB11 dolls.
